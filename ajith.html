ajith kumar history

Ajith Kumar is one of the most prominent and bankable actors in Tamil cinema, famously known by his mononym Ajith or fan-given titles like "Thala" and "Ultimate Star". Beyond his massive cinematic legacy, he is a dedicated professional race car driver and was awarded the Padma Bhushan in 2025.Born on May 1, 1971, in Secunderabad (present-day Telangana) to a Tamil father and Sindhi mother, Ajith was raised in Chennai. He did not come from a film background and began working odd jobs, such as an auto mechanic and merchandiser, to support his passion for motorsports. He started his modeling career by chance after being approached at a racetrack to fund his racing dreams.Ajith made his official acting debut in the 1993 Telugu film Prema Pusthakam, followed closely by his Tamil debut in Amaravathi (1993). His breakthrough arrived with the 1995 romantic thriller Aasai. Over a career spanning more than three decades, he has starred in over 60 films, solidifying his superstardom through iconic hits such as Kadhal Kottai, Vali, Dheena, Billa, Mankatha, and Viswasam.Simultaneously, Ajith has maintained a highly successful career as an occasional racing driver. He transitioned to Formula racing, competing in the Asian Formula BMW Championship, the British Formula 3 Scholarship Class, and the prestigious FIA Formula 2 Championship. He continues to represent India on the international stage, managing his own professional racing outfit, Ajith Kumar Racing.
